In the Observer design pattern, there are two main components, Observable and Observer.
Observer is to observe Observable. Whenever there is any change in state of Observable,
it will update all its Observers about it's state change to perform their task.

We will be solving NotifyMe of Amazon/Flipkart on products availability to implement this
design pattern.

Click on NotifyMe, it will notify us whenever the particular product is available in stock
again.
